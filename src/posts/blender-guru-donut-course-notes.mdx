and coffee.

## Part 1

You initial land on the grid canvas known as the **3d viewport** where
you will spend most of your time.

### Moving objects

Select the move icon in the lefthand toolbar.

- Move along an axis with arrows
- Click the circle to move multiple axis at once

### Rotating objects

Select the rotate icon in the lefthand toolbar.

- Rotate along an axis by clicking a line
- Click the object itself to rotate along all axis at once

### Canceling actions

Right click with your mouse and it will reset the object when
you're in the middle of rotating/moving/etc.

### Scaling objects

Select the scale icon in the lefthand toolbar.

- Scale along an axis by clicking a line
- Click the object itself to scale along all axis at once

### Hotkeys

- <kbd>g</kbd>: Move, "g for grab"
  - <kbd>x</kbd>, <kbd>y</kbd>, <kbd>z</kbd>: pin an axis for movement
  - Middle mouse button: Snap to axis
- <kbd>r</kbd>: Rotate
  - <kbd>x</kbd>, <kbd>y</kbd>, <kbd>z</kbd>: pin an axis for rotation
  - Middle mouse button: Snap to axis
- <kbd>s</kbd>: Scale
  - <kbd>x</kbd>, <kbd>y</kbd>, <kbd>z</kbd>: pin an axis for scaling
  - Middle mouse button: Snap to axis
